Fantasy Fighters

Chapter I

You suddenly come to your senses and find yourself in a dusty place. Once you settle down, you realise that it is an arena, and you are wearing heavy armor ready with a sword in your right hand and a shield in your left. How did you end up here? A battle-hardened man exclaims to you "I am going to crush you!!, no one messes with The Strange Stranger." You have no choice but to fight for survival.
